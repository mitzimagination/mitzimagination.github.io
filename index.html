Water Found on Mars!

Do you love robots that explore outer space? Subscribe to our newsletter Bots Beyond.
It's been known for years that water existed at the poles of Mars, but now, the industrious rover, Curiosity, has found evidence of flowing water on the Red Planet.

Curiosity was launched in November of 2011 and landed in a spectacular fashion in August 2012. Using a hovering, manueverable rocket called a Sky Crane, the rover was delicately placed on Mars in a multi-stage ballet of specifically timed engine burns and bungie-like cables.
